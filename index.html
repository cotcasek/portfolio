About Me

I’m Clare (she/her), data scientist and cartographer, residing in the majestic Pacific Northwest. I am a graduate of the University of Oregon, and have recently completed a Bachelor's degree in Data Science and Geography from the University of Oregon. During my time at U of O, I was introduced to the world of cartography and focused my techincal coursework on cartographic projects. I continue to work in the Infographics Lab at the university, supporting a number of projects - including the migration focused web maps highlighted below.

I enjoy sharing stories visually through maps and data, focusing my work on conservation, public lands, art, and expression. I have experience in web mapping, print products, data processing and visualization, and computer programming. Building on a background in project management, business, and engineering, I bring a practical and systems-oriented perspective to my work in the geospatial field.
