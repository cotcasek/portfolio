About Me

I’m Clare Otcasek (she/her), data scientist and cartographer, residing in the majestic Pacific Northwest. I enjoy sharing stories visually through maps and data, focusing my work on conservation, public lands, art, and expression. I have experience in web mapping, print products, data processing and visualization, and computer programming. Building on a background in project management, business, and engineering, I bring a practical and systems-oriented perspective to my work in the geospatial field.
